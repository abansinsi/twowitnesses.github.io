Two Witnesses Outreach

Two witnesses outreach is a non-denominational christian gathering located in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria with Prophet Godwin Michael Udoh as the founder and General overseer. It is a teaching, healing and deliverance ministry. The ministry, Two Witnesses Outreach started as a non-denominational christian gathering at No 9 Umoh Uko Street off IBB Avenue, Uyo, Akwa Ibom State on April 2007 as a night vigil and grew to where it is now, at Mbierebe Obio Off Aka Etinan Road, Uyo

We have recorded tremendous growth in ministry as deliverance and healing are conducted for as many as are oppressed by the devil as God uses his servant immensely. Many poor and wretched people are loosed from satanic bonds and made wealthy. God is using his servant mightily to heal many infirmities such as HIV, barenness, cancer, issue of blood, madness etc . However, Prophet Godwin Michael Udoh, was divinely warned not to disclose to the world his calling which is like Noah's ministry in the bible, to give the closing message to the world until the appointed time comes, Hence, the vision was kept sealed.

God has now instructed His servant, Prophet Godwin Michael Udoh to make himself known to the world as the prophet He has chosen to give the last gospel message to the world before Christ's glorious second advent. God proves this call with unarguable signs and wonders which includes: the raising back to life of many deceased people, some of which happened over the phone, the healing of various diseases and deliverance from demonic oppression. The future of the ministry lies in reaching out to the unreached with this message for all round preparation against the day of God's judgement.
